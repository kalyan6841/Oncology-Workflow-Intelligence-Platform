# Oncology Workflow Intelligence Platform

## Overview

The Oncology Workflow Intelligence Platform is an AI-assisted oncology workflow coordination platform designed to support treatment planning, chemotherapy scheduling, laboratory coordination, infusion resource optimization, pharmacy workflow management, patient engagement, and operational decision support.

This project was inspired by real world challenges observed during clinical oncology practice and explores how data-driven systems can improve cancer care coordination while preserving clinician led decision making.


## Clinical Problem

Cancer treatment involves multiple stakeholders including oncologists, clinical pharmacists, nursing teams, laboratory personnel, pharmacy services, daycare units, administrators, and patients.

Delays in communication, treatment readiness assessment, laboratory review, infusion scheduling, and operational coordination can impact treatment continuity and healthcare efficiency.


 Project Objectives

* Improve chemotherapy workflow coordination
* Support treatment readiness assessment
* Optimize infusion chair utilization
* Enhance laboratory coordination
* Improve pharmacy preparation workflows
* Strengthen patient engagement and follow-up
* Explore predictive analytics for operational decision support

## Clinical Safety Statement

This platform is intended as a workflow coordination and decision-support tool.

It does not replace clinical judgement, oncologist decision making, or institutional treatment protocols.

All treatment decisions remain clinician controlled.
